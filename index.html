About Vincent Grégoire

Vincent Grégoire joined The University of Melbourne in 2013 as an Assistant Professor (called Senior Lecturer in Australia) after obtaining a Ph.D. in Finance from The University of British Columbia.

His main research interests are in asset pricing, mutual funds, market microstructure, big data, financial technologies and cryptocurrencies.
